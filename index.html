         ABOUT ME

My name is BOI DANQUAH BERTRAM, I come from Kwame Danso, located in the Bone East region of Ghana. I am a boy of nineteen years of age, I started my primary school from the Seventh Day Adventist known as SDA. I stopped schooling in that school and I continued my schooling in Zephonel Educational Complex.

After I completed school in Zephonel Educational Complex, I was enrolled in to Ameyaw Senior High And Technical School, located in Techiman in the Bono East Region of Ghana. I was given the chance to study general science as my program of study. I was able to comply by the rules and regulations of the school, I did my possible best as required as a student and God being on my side, I was able to make all the necessary grades after I wrote my final exams in my second cycle of education. I didn't find it easy although, because life wasn't easy by then. Glory to HIS name that i didn't perish. I was able to overcome all the challenges and all the afflictions that came my way.

After i completed my second cycle education, I had the opportunity to enter into the university in that same year. I was enrolled in to the University of Ghana to study BSc physical science and I belong to the collage of basic and applied sciences.

And here in the university,I was told. I was told by most people that life in university is simple, enjoyable, and many other things, they are all true although, but not to how they told me, its true, that you can decide to go for lectures, you can decide not to go, no one would ever force you to go for lectures

Upon all the stress in the university, we I am still doing my best to make sure I get myself fixed in it to make me acheive my aims and goals as well and I hope that the Lord that paved a way for me to enter into this university would continued to be with me to have a successful stay and a better class class.
